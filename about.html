About Us

This is a project for WEHack 2022. As women in engineering disciplines, we know how difficult it can be to find a community. We designed this page to aid you in finding your people. Check out the Clubs page to learn about all the different women-focused organizations at UTD, like SWE or WWC. Head to the Calendar to check out when and where upcoming events are. Under the mentorship tab, you can fill out a form to become a mentee or a mentor and find other women to help or be helped by!
